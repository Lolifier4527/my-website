What It Is

One system.
Seven fields.

The PatchLab Rugby Score Capture system is a web-based tool that allows tournament organisers to capture live scores from up to 7 fields simultaneously — with each score instantly displayed on a dedicated scoreboard screen, no app installation required.

Built for schools, clubs, and rugby unions running multi-field tournaments, it replaces manual score sheets and delayed announcements with a clean, professional digital experience.

Separate score capture form per field (A through G)
Scoreboard auto-updates the moment a score is entered

Built-in match timer per field

Half Time Control

Blank the board.
Resume instantly.

Each field's score operator has a dedicated BLANK SCOREBOARD button. During half time, one tap blacks out that field's display — showing a pulsing "HALF TIME" message on the big screen. Tap again to resume instantly when play restarts.

How It Works

Set up in minutes.

01
Open the Score Capture App

The tournament coordinator opens the landing page and selects a field — or shares a direct QR code link with each score operator. No logins, no passwords.

02
Enter Team Names & Start the Timer

The operator enters the home and away team names, selects the age group, and starts the match timer. The scoreboard comes alive immediately.

03
Tap to Score

Every try, conversion, penalty and drop kick is a single tap. Scores update on the big screen scoreboard in real time — no refreshing needed.

04
Half Time & Full Time

Tap the BLANK button at half time to black out the scoreboard. At full time, submit the match — the system prompts to email the result to the administrator.

05
Review & Export

All matches are saved in the History tab. Export the full tournament results as a CSV file for use in spreadsheets or official records.
